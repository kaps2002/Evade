About Us

Hey everyone, we at Evade are so excited to present our product. Evade is a web based application that lets users check how many towers do they have in the vicinity of their place. Evade works only for indian addresses for the time being, but we're planning to extend it for more countries. Making evade was very much fun and enjoying. For designing the web application, we used Figma. For making the app, we used flask that served as our default wsgi server, basic HTML-CSS for frontend, and heroku hosting for deploying the app on web. It uses the data from a csv file that we got from OpenCelliD API, so many a thanks to them, and then we ran our query through the latitude and longitude to let users know how many towers do they have around them. Also, users can know about their risk level, and can learn about preventive measures that they can follow to ensure that they're safe from the supposedly harmful radiations from cell towers.
